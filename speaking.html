Speaking

Carol enjoys giving lectures and presentations, speaking on panels, and leading workshops on many topics including: user experience, human-computer interaction, information architecture, human-machine teaming, and sharing views on acquiring, developing, and maintaining AI systems.

She has given over 250 talks across 45+ cities since 2004. Carol is an ACM Distinguished Speaker (2021–2025), IEEE senior member, and UXPA member. For a detailed list of publications, view her Google Scholar page.
